About Us

Aesthetics BioMedical, Inc. is an aesthetic innovation company uniquely offering procedure and product enhancement, development, clinical indication expansion, application optimization, FDA clearance expertise, and worldwide distribution solutions for entrepreneurs, domestic, and international companies.

Aesthetics BioMedical’s innovation center is a leader in breakthrough technologies and multiple source combination therapies for our clients, physician network, and the aesthetic arena. We welcome you and your company to partner with us to further the field of aesthetics.

Our Mission

Aesthetics BioMedical is committed to the development and distribution of innovative aesthetic devices, products, and services for the global aesthetic market. We integrate state-of-the-art education and training to guarantee the best possible outcomes for our clients and their patients.

Our ongoing support ensures that our customers’ every need is met - from device integration to training support for the life of our relationship. We also innovate new models for the advancement of aesthetic medical practices.

MaryAnn Guerra, MBA
Co-Founderclose

MaryAnn is co-founder of Aesthetics BioMedical with Dr. Farhan Taghizadeh. Aesthetics Biomedical is a new start-up company dedicated to the delivery of aesthetic services utilizing through trust source physician networks. ABM will offer the latest products combined with novel devices to ensure best, extended outcomes for customers at pricing they can afford. She is also serving as Chairman of the Board, CEO, and co-founder of BioAccel, a non-profit organization located in Phoenix, Arizona. Ms. Guerra is known for creating novel programs to accelerate the commercialization of technology from the lab into new business opportunities. Ms. Guerra spent much of her career operating successful and progressive health, science, technology and start up businesses. Since the launch of BioAccel in April 2009, 17 companies have been successfully launched with products already commercially available in the US, Europe and Mexico. BioAccel has partnered with the City of Peoria to create the first medical device accelerator, embedding the BioAccel model into its operations to ensure positive economic impact. In February 2016 BioAccel received its second i6 Regional Seed Grant Award to establish 3 new venture funds in the southwest with MaryAnn leading this effort. In April 2015 BioAccel received it first i6 Innovation Challenge Award, also the first time any organization in Arizona received this grant. In 2012 BioAccel received the State Science and Technology Institutes’ most Innovative New Initiative Award. Prior to founding BioAccel, Ms. Guerra served as President of TGen Accelerators, LLC and Chief Operating Officer, TGen. MaryAnn has also had an impressive career at the National Institutes of Health having held senior level positions, including: Executive Officer, National Heart Lung and Blood Institute and Deputy Director of Management & Executive Officer at the National Cancer Institute.

Tim Shanahan, Vice President of Salesclose

Mr. Shanahan brings 25 seasoned years of executive management, sales, marketing, and master global distribution experience within the medical sales industry. He has consulted for numerous entities (Lumenis Ltd., Fotona, etc.) regarding medical aesthetic selling, marketing, training, revenue capturing, distributing, and expanding business opportunities through venture capital alliances and acquisition partnering. His recent experiences include: Vice President of Sales for BiO2, a cosmeceutical wound healing manufacturer of Proven ScarRelief® and Amino-Plex®, CEO of Revenue Cure Inc., an aesthetic brokerage where he brokered the distribution deal for BiO2 with Omni Bioceutical Innovations and consulted for Viora and EpiMedica Inc. Former experiences include: Director of Sales for Healthcare Finance Direct which offers an in-house patient financing program, Vice President of Sales for FitAID which manufactures a unique fitness recovery beverage, Director of Sales for Hoya ConBio in their medical aesthetic laser division now acquired by Cynosure, Inc., various sale positions at the medical laser device companies Palomar, Cynosure, Lutronic, and Zoll Medical Corporation which is a leader in defibrillators and external pacemakers. Tim’s unique, innovative entrepreneurial style fits right into Aesthetics BioMedical Inc.’s framework as “The Uniquely Innovative Aesthetic Company.”
