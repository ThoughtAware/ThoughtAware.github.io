VIVACE

The New Generation of Advanced Micro-Needling RF

  • Micro-Needling Multi-Level RF Syste
  • Most Comfortable Client Experience
  • Best Priced Microneedling RF Syste
  • 31 Multi-Level Precise Depths
  • Robotic Soothe GLide Handpiece for Gentle Insertion
  • 1 MHz Radiofrequency
  • 36 Gold, Perfectly Sized Insulated Needles
  • 70 Watts
  • Red and Blue LED
  • AVC (Automatic Visualization Confirmation

The Vivace device is a revolutionary combination of radio frequency and microneedling. The device works by incorporating radio frequency energy into each individual needle, driving the rejuvenating ​energy deep into the skin and stimulating the healthy growth of new collagen. This minimally invasive procedure is nearly painless with the use of a topical numbing agent and can be performed in less than one hour per treatment.

Our ongoing support ensures that our customers’ every need is met - from device integration to training support for the life of our relationship. We also innovate new models for the advancement of aesthetic medical practices.
